Fish & Chips
Ingredient
Instructions
For the Fish
  • 1 lb white fish fillets (cod, haddock, or halibut)
  • Salt and pepper, to season the fish
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, for dredging
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or canola oil works well)
For the Batter:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup cold beer (or sparkling water for a non-alcoholic option)
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for extra crispiness)
For the Chips
  • 2 large resset potatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying(use the same oil as for the fish)
Optional Tartar Sauce
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp pickles, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp capers, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Prepare the Potatoes(Chips):
  • Peel the potatoes and cut them into 1/4-inch thick slices or strips for fries.
  • Place the potato slices in cold water and let them soak for 30 minutes. This helps remove excess starch for extra crispy fries.
  • Drain and pat the potatoes dry with paper towels.
Prepare the Batter:
  • Peel the potatoes and cut them into 1/4-inch thick slices or strips for fries.
  • Place the potato slices in cold water and let them soak for 30 minutes. This helps remove excess starch for extra crispy fries.
  • Drain and pat the potatoes dry with paper towels.
    • First Fry of the Chips:
  • Heat oil in a deep fryer or large, heavy-bottomed pot to 325°F (163°C).
  • Fry the potato slices in batches for 2-3 minutes, until they are soft but not browned. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  • Increase oil temperature to 375°F (190°C) for the second fry.
    • Prepare the Fish
    • Pat the fish fillets dry with paper towels and season with salt and pepper.
    • Lightly dredge each fillet in flour, shaking off the excess. This helps the batter stick.
    Fry the Fish:
    • Dip each floured fillet into the cold batter, letting any excess drip off.
    • Carefully place the fish in the hot oil (375°F / 190°C) and fry for about 4-5 minutes, until golden and crispy. Work in batches if necessary.
    • Remove the fish and drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt while hot.
    Second Fry of the Chips:
    • Return the pre-fried chips to the hot oil (375°F / 190°C) and fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy.
    • Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
    Optional Tartar Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, chopped pickles, capers, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well and serve on the side.
